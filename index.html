You Might Ask

Frequently Asked Questions

1 What type of cleaning services do you offer?
We offer a range of cleaning services, including residential cleaning, commercial cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, and more. We can customize our cleaning services to meet your specific needs.
2 Are your cleaners trained and insured?
Yes, all our cleaners are highly trained and experienced. We also carry liability insurance to protect our clients in the event of any damage or accidents.
3 What cleaning products do you use?
We use eco-friendly cleaning products and equipment to minimize our impact on the environment. If you have any specific preferences or requirements, we'll work with you to accommodate them.
4 How do I schedule a cleaning service?
You can schedule a cleaning service by contacting us via phone, email, or our website. We'll work with you to find a convenient time and date for your cleaning service.
5 Do I need to be present during the cleaning?
No, you don't need to be present during the cleaning. Many of our clients prefer to be away during the cleaning, but if you'd like to stay, you're more than welcome to do so.
